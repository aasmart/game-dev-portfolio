Custom Feature

I co-developoed a new ground pound system for Metroid in addition to a suite of custom levels to explore this feature. This vision for this feature was to enhance combat and mobility

  • Peform an attack that launches the player downward at high speeds
  • Bounce off grounded enemies (in addition to Rippers) for extra height and interesting movement
  • Make strategic decisions between normal attacks and ground pounding
